
Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platformer developed by RobTop Games. Players control a customizable icon that moves automatically across the screen. The primary goal is to navigate through a series of obstacles, such as spikes and walls, without crashing.

The gameplay relies heavily on musical synchronization. Users must time their jumps and movements to match the beat of the soundtrack. Each level presents a unique layout and difficulty setting, requiring focus and quick reflexes to complete successfully.
