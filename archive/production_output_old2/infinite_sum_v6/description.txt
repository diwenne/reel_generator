
"Never Gonna Give You Up" is the debut single recorded by English singer and songwriter Rick Astley, originally released in 1987. The song became a worldwide number-one hit, topping the charts in twenty-five countries, and remains the artist's most recognizable work. The accompanying music video features Astley performing the song in various locations, including an empty ballroom and an underpass.

In the digital age, this music video has gained renewed significance through the internet phenomenon known as "Rickrolling." This bait-and-switch practice involves linking the video unexpectedly in online discussions.
