
Cataclysm is a well-known Extreme Demon level in Geometry Dash, created by the user Ggb0y. Historically, this level is significant because it helped define the Extreme Demon difficulty standard upon its release. It is characterized by its intense red and black color scheme, which sets a distinct and intimidating atmosphere for the player.

The gameplay requires precise timing and consistency, featuring challenging ship and wave sequences that leave little margin for error. Completing this level with 100% accuracy and collecting all three coins is regarded as a substantial milestone within the Geometry Dash community. It remains a classic test of skill for experienced players looking to prove their abilities.
