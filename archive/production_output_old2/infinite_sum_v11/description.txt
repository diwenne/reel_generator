
Cataclysm is a well-known Extreme Demon level in Geometry Dash, created and published by the player Ggb0y. It is widely recognized for establishing the "Hell" theme trend within the game, characterized by its intense red and black color scheme. The level presents a significant challenge to players, featuring tight wave sections and precise ship maneuvers.

Completing this level 100% requires extensive practice and strict timing. It remains a historic milestone in the Geometry Dash community, marking a shift in difficulty standards for Demon levels. This run showcases a successful completion of the map, including the collection of three user coins.
