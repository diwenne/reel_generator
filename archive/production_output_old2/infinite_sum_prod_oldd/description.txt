
Cataclysm is widely recognized as one of the most iconic Extreme Demons in Geometry Dash history. Created by Ggb0y, the level features a distinct red and black visual theme and is known for its incredibly tight wave sections and precise timing. For many competitive players, completing this level serves as a significant milestone in their progression.

This level famously initiated a new era of difficulty within the game and serves as the first installment of the "Apocalyptic Trilogy," paving the way for successors like Bloodbath and Aftermath. Mastering the gameplay mechanics required to reach 100% completion demands extensive practice and unwavering consistency.
