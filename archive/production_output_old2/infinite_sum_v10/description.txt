
Cataclysm is a renowned custom level in Geometry Dash, created by the user Ggb0y. It is classified as an Extreme Demon, representing one of the highest difficulty tiers available in the game. Known for its distinct red and black aesthetic, the level challenges players with tight flying sections and complex timing.

Historically, Cataclysm is significant because it helped define the standards for extreme difficulty during the game's 1.9 update era. It stands as the first installment in the famous "Apocalyptic Trilogy," preceding the levels Bloodbath and Aftermath. Achieving 100% completion with all three coins requires exceptional skill and consistency.
