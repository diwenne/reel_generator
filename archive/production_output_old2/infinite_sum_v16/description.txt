
Cataclysm is a renowned custom level in Geometry Dash created by the player Ggb0y. Rated as an Extreme Demon, it is widely recognized for its high level of difficulty and precise timing requirements. The level utilizes a distinct red and black visual style, often referred to as a "Hell" theme, which became a standard aesthetic for many subsequent extreme levels.

Historically, Cataclysm is notable for starting a trend of increasingly difficult challenges within the game. It serves as the direct predecessor to other famous levels like Bloodbath and Aftermath. Completing this level 100% and collecting all three user coins requires extensive practice and represents a significant achievement of skill within the Geometry Dash community.
